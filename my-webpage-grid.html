Johannesburg Historical Places

Goldreef City

Gold Reef City is an iconic amusement park and entertainment complex located in Johannesburg, South Africa. It's built on the site of an old gold mine and pays homage to the city's gold rush era, offering visitors a combination of thrilling rides, historical attractions, and various forms of entertainment.

Newtown

Newtown is a vibrant cultural and entertainment district located in Johannesburg, South Africa. It's known for its rich history, diverse community, and thriving arts scene. Originally established in the late 19th century, Newtown has undergone significant revitalization in recent years, transforming into a dynamic hub for creativity, innovation, and social engagement.

Soweto Towers

The Soweto Towers, also known as the Orlando Towers, are iconic landmarks located in the township of Soweto, near Johannesburg, South Africa. These twin cooling towers were originally part of the Orlando Power Station, which was decommissioned in the 1990s. Instead of being demolished, the towers were transformed into a unique tourist attraction and symbol of urban renewal.
